Thirty Posts in Thirty Days

Sep 20th, 2009

Happy one year anniversary!  As Chris Hammond pointed out this week, it has been exactly one year since I last made a post to my blog – this blog!

It is not for a lack of content.  I have well over 100 of “should have, will do, todos, blog this” of bookmarks, code snippets, and more.  It didn’t ring a bell until christoc actually pinged me this week to tell me. 

So, today I start a new goal: Thirty posts in thirty days.  Now, there may be several posts in one day in succession, and other empty days.  But, there will be at least 30.  :)
