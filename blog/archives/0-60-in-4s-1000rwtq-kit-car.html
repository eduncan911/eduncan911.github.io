You're eyes aren't lying… Meet the Tango, an electric commuter car available by mail order, assembly required.

http://www.commutercars.com/

It has 1000 ft.tq. and can do 0-60 in 4s.  And you can park it on the sidewalk…

Did I mention the $85,000 cost for this kit-car? 

They mention it as being ultra-stable, mainly due to over 80% of the wieght is a few inches off of the ground (the batteries and DC motors).  Check out this video of them at an Autocross:

http://www.commutercars.com/images/theater/marinaAutocross/marina.rm

I'll take one.
