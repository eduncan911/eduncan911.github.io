Deja Vu, a Gamble

Aug 21st, 2005

I really think this phenomenon is annoying.  Time and time again, I see something that I could swear I saw before.  A car, a movie, a place I could swear I’ve been to before, a book that I haven’t read but seem to remember bits. 

I want to have words with the person who planted the seed in my head that, “Deja Vu is simply where one side of your brain processes something faster then the other side.  So you have a feeling that you’ve been there before, or seen that person before.”  I think it was in a movie and I want to speak to that writer. 

Today, it was another one of those “I’ve seen that person before”.  But this time, it was both a woman and a guy that looked familiar (didn’t hurt that she was very attractive for me to notice).

So I sat here in Panera, looking over the notebook playing eye-swap with her across the room.  “I know this woman from somewhere.  That guy with her looks familiar too.  Do I say something, do I leave it?  They haven’t recognized me, but I think I know them.  Do I just leave it cause I could be wrong?  Do I say something in case I am right?  Screw it, I do know them.”
